Face Compare

Comparison of faces can be quite challenging, especially when a face has makeup or other elements, covering the real nature of it. To offset this challenge, we use our AI solutions to recognize and analyze the key attributes of a face in an image, video, and even in real-time. the face compare feature can help you identify people, verify faces, and look for faces from those stored in the database.

We combine machine learning algorithms with the face descriptors to perform the comparison of two faces. to get the best result possible for the face comparison, we leverage Convolutional Neural Networks that have a huge database with a number of facial images marked per identify.
